VIRAT KOHLI

The young and talented right-handed batsman, Virat Kohli, was born on the 5th of November, 1988, in Delhi. His father, Prem Kohli, was a lawyer who passed away on December 19th, 2006, due to a brain stroke. Virat has an elder brother, Vikash, and an elder sister, Bhavna. He attended St. Sofia School in Paschim Vihar, Delhi. Virat Kohli was among the first intake of The West Delhi Cricket Academy, created in 1998.
